About Reflectory

Who we are

Reflectory is a student-first mentorship foundation focused on clear, evidence-based career guidance and practical life skills. We combine human empathy with AI-assisted planning — always reviewed by mentors.

Our Vision

A future where every student chooses a career aligned with abilities and values.

Our Mission

Provide accessible, ethical mentorship combining human oversight and AI tools.

Values

  • Clarity: Transparent, data-informed guidance.
  • Compassion: Emotional safety and support.
  • Ethics: Prioritise consent & privacy.
  • Growth: Habit-based skill building.
Legal & Ethical

No individual consulting for minors under 18 without parental consent. AI outputs are reviewed by human mentors.

Evidence-Based

Psychometric inputs + structured interviewing to recommend practical paths.

Programs

Reflectory offers programs for career clarity, practical skills and emotional resilience.

Personal Mentorship

One-on-one mentorship for mature students (18+). Tailored sessions for decision-making, goal-setting, and progress tracking. Parental consent required for those under 18.

Career Clarity

Psychometric assessments + AI planning to map realistic career & education paths.

Skill Development

Short courses on productivity, communication, public speaking, and disciplined habit formation.

Emotional Support Circles

Peer-led groups for resilience and perspective-sharing. Facilitators are trained peer mentors (not therapists).
