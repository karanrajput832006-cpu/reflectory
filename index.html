About Reflectory

Who we are

Reflectory is a student-first mentorship foundation focused on delivering clear, evidence-based career guidance and practical life skills. We help students understand their strengths, explore options, and build the habits required to transition from uncertainty to confident decision-making.

Our Vision

A future where every student can choose a meaningful career and life path aligned with their abilities and values.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, ethical, and high-quality mentorship combining human empathy with AI-assisted planning.

Values

  • Clarity: Data-informed and transparent guidance.
  • Compassion: Emotional safety and peer support.
  • Ethics: Prioritise consent, privacy, and legal compliance.
  • Growth: Skill building and lifelong learning focus.
Legal & Ethical

We do not provide individual consulting to minors (under 18) without parental consent. We comply with applicable rules and maintain confidentiality.

Reflectory uses AI tools such as ChatGPT to help mentors prepare schedules and insights. All AI content is reviewed by human mentors before sharing with students. No automated decisions or sensitive data storage is done using AI.

Evidence-Based

We use psychometric inputs, structured interviews, and AI-assisted planning tools to guide recommendations.

Programs

Reflectory offers a set of carefully designed programs to address career clarity, practical skills, and emotional resilience.

Personal Mentorship

One-on-one mentorship for mature students (18+). Tailored sessions focusing on decision-making, goal setting and progress tracking. Note: parental consent required for those under 18.

Career Clarity

Psychometric assessments combined with AI-based planning to map career options, course choices, and realistic pathways.

Skill Development

Short courses and guided practice on productivity, communication, public speaking, and disciplined habit formation.

Emotional Support Circles

Peer-led group conversations for emotional resilience and perspective-sharing. These are supportive spaces, not therapy — facilitators are trained peer mentors.

Reflectory provides mentorship and educational guidance only. We do not offer therapy, medical, legal, or financial advice. Individual results may vary based on effort and participation.

Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 2025

Reflectory is a student-first mentorship foundation based in Palanpur, Gujarat, India. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling all personal information responsibly and transparently, in full compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP 2023).

1️⃣ Who We Are

Reflectory is an MSME-registered mentorship foundation providing ethical, evidence-based guidance and life skills for students. Our programs combine human mentorship with AI-assisted insights — always reviewed and approved by mentors before sharing with students.

2️⃣ What Data We Collect

  • Name, age, contact details (email, phone)
  • Educational background and academic preferences
  • Responses to mentoring forms, questionnaires, or feedback
  • Parental or guardian details (for minors under 18)
  • Communication records (emails, chat history, or form submissions)

3️⃣ How We Use Your Data

Your personal information is used only for providing mentorship, improving programs, responding to queries, and complying with legal requirements. We do not sell, rent, or share your data for commercial gain.

4️⃣ AI Tools & Human Oversight

Reflectory uses AI tools such as ChatGPT to assist mentors with insights and scheduling. All AI-generated content is reviewed by a human mentor and not used for automated decision-making.

5️⃣ Data Retention & Deletion

We retain personal data for up to 12 months after program completion; thereafter data is deleted or anonymized unless you request otherwise.

6️⃣ Contact

For data requests, contact: reflectory.in@gmail.com
